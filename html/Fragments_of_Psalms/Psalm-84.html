Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 84

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

Conuerte nos, deus salutaris noster, et auerte iram tuam a nobis.
Gehweorf us hraðe,         hælend drihten,
and ðin yrre fram us         eac oncyrre.
